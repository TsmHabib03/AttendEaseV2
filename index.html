About San Francisco High School

San Francisco High School is one of the largest public secondary schools in Quezon City and continues to provide accessible, quality education for junior and senior high school learners.

  • Established June 9, 1954
  • Former Name Don Mariano Marcos High School (1975-1986)
  • School Level Public Secondary School (Grades 7-12)
  • Location Misamis Street, Sto. Cristo, Quezon City
